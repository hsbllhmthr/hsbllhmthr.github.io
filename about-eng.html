About Me

Yolooo what's up guys 🖐, let me introduce myself, I'm Hasbullah Dedat Hasbala M (the 'M' is my family name, Mathar). I was born in Ujung Pandang city, which is now better known as Makassar city. Currently, I'm in the final stage of completing my undergraduate degree in Computer Science 👨‍💻 at Alauddin State Islamic University in Makassar. Fortunately, my passion for technology goes hand in hand with my major.

Since I was in elementary school, I have been interacting with PCs or computers that still use old versions of Windows, such as Windows XP or Vista. My interest in tinkering with computers continued with hobbies in graphic design (multimedia), which led me to participate in competitions during high school 🪑.

My dislike for mathematics made me end up in this major, but it was a decision I made to pursue Computer Science.

During my college years, I got to know many people who are involved in the IT industry. I realized that this field is much broader than I had previously imagined and that my knowledge was still far behind theirs. It was during that time that I got to know some application developers, and I took it as a lesson to develop my skills in the IT industry

I learned a lot from various communities 👨‍👦‍👦 of application developers including the Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) by Google Developers and became part of the Core Team from 2022 to 2023. After participating in various GDSC activities, I also took part in events organized by the Google Developer Groups (GDG) Makassar.

During my college years, I received several scholarships in various forms, one of which was a scholarship in the IT field called Bangkit Academy 2023 By Google, GoTo, Traveloka Google Cloud ☁ Learning Path which greatly helped me to learn about the world of technology and application development. Bangkit is a career readiness program aimed at producing highly skilled technical talent for world-class Indonesian technology companies and startups, fully supported by Google, GoTo, and Traveloka. Bangkit has successfully trained 6,400 students from all over Indonesia.

In addition to my studies, I also entered the professional world, and alhamdulillah, I was able to earn money 💸 as a programmer and graphic designer since the beginning of my college years at elfatafoundation.com. I was responsible for several features and mostly handled the design for the media creative team and still working as a freelancer to get side jobs.
